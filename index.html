Types of beer
Lager - This beer is brewed and conditioned at low temperature. It is light, crisp and easy to drink.
Ale - This beer is brewer using a warm fermentation method, resulting in a sweet, full-bodied and fuity taste.
Stout - A very dark, full-bodied ale with a distinctive malty flavor that is typically brewed with unmalted roasted barley.
Wheat beer - This is a top fermented beer which is brewed with a large proportion of wheat. They are hazy, citrusy and have fuller mouthfeel.
